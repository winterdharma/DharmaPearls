<h1><a href='../index.html'>The Related Discourses</a></h1>
<h2><a href='index.html'>5. Elements</a></h2>

<table class="trans">
  <th class='ch'>（四四九） 五戒</th>
  <th class='en'>6 (449). The Five Precepts</th>
  <tr>
    <td class='ch' title='t99.2.115c6'>如是我聞： 一時，佛住舍衛國、祇樹、給孤獨園。</td>
    <td id='p1'>1. Thus I have heard:<sup id="ref1"><a href="#n1">1</a></sup> One time, the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in Jeta’s Grove of Śrāvastī.</td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td class='ch' title='t99.2.115c7'>爾時，世尊告諸比丘： 「眾生常與界俱，與界和合⋯」 如是廣說乃至⋯ 「勝心生時與勝界俱。 鄙心生時與鄙界俱。</td>
    <td id='p2'>2. It was then that the Bhagavān addressed the monks, “Sentient beings always associate with their elements, and those with those elements come together … <em>up to</em> … When their thoughts are greater, they associate with greater elements. When their thoughts are lowly, they associate with lowly elements.</td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td class='ch' title='t99.2.115c9'>「殺生時與殺界俱。 盜⋯婬⋯妄語⋯飲酒心時，與飲酒界俱。 不殺生時與不殺界俱。 不盜⋯不婬⋯不妄語⋯不飲酒，與不飲酒界俱。</td>
    <td id='p3'>3. “When they kill beings, they associate with the element of killing.<sup id="ref2"><a href="#n2">2</a></sup> When they think of stealing … having [wrong] sex … speaking falsely … drinking alcohol, they associate with the element of drinking alcohol. When they don’t kill beings, they associate with the element of not killing beings. When they don’t steal … don’t have [wrong] sex … don’t speak falsely … don’t drink alcohol, they associate with the element of not drinking alcohol.</td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td class='ch' title='t99.2.115c12'>「是故，諸比丘，當善分別種種界。」</td>
    <td id='p4'>4. “Therefore, monks, you should discern the various elements well.”</td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td class='ch' title='t99.2.115c13'>佛說是經已，諸比丘聞佛所說歡喜，奉行。</td>
    <td id='p5'>5. After the Buddha spoke this <em>sūtra</em>, the monks who heard what the Buddha taught rejoiced and approved.</td>
  </tr>
</table>
